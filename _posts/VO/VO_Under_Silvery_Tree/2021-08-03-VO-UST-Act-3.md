*The furthest distance in the world*

*Is not being apart while being in love*

*But when plainly cannot resist the yearning*

*Yet pretending you have never been in my heart*

<br>

世界上最遥远的距离

不是相爱的人彼此分离

而是明知爱到痴迷

却装作毫不在意
