我用一个长筒望远镜隔着很远的距离窥视一个在学校里的人，然后被那里的人当成狙击枪，所以要来追杀我。我从一堵有点高的砖墙上直接往下跳，然后躲在墙角下，听见搜捕我的人在后面走动。我屏着呼吸等着，怕被发现。

<br>

（英文版本：）

I was using a long telescope to observe someone/something in a school, but people there thought it was a sniper rifle and they were coming after me. I remember jumping from some tall walls and hiding beneath it, just like some action movies. I could hear them moving closer, examining inch by inch, and I just hid there, stayed very still and hoped they wouldn't find me.
