Probably

One of the biggest mysteries

Love can't fix everything

Can't heal trauma

Not always

Not all the times

<br>

Sometimes

Trauma comes from love, even

Sometimes

With all the love in the world

Trauma is still there

<br>

Healing

Slowly

Glacially

But still there

<br>

Deep

Old

There
