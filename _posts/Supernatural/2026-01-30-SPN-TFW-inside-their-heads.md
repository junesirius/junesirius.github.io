It's funny to think all three of them, Dean, Sam, Cas, have been possessed by either angel or demon and forced to live inside their own heads during the possession time, and that the other two would try to find some help (usually Crowley) to go inside their heads and wake them up. Unlike how people would pretend on the outside, it is not unreasonable to assume that they're their truest self when they're inside, which is probably the most perfect time to peek inside their heads (literally) and do some character study about what they really are like if they're completely alone and don't have to think about anything in the outside world.

(Mary too has such experience, but let's focus on TFW for now)
