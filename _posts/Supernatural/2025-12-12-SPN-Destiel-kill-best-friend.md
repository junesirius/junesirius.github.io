had a dream recently of almost having my best friend killed, all bloody and desperate and guilty and I woke up crying, still completely devastated and wrecked

And in the dream, I believed I should be the one who got killed instead, I didn't deserve to be forgiven for making such big mistake, and worst of all, I wanted to tell my best friend I love them. But I woke up before the dream-me could say it or hear what they'd answer. It messed me up even worse than the bloody dream already was.

After waking up, I wanted so desperately to see my friend, to talk to them, to know they're okay, but also not trusting myself to approach them, fear that I might actually cause them harm, so I'd hold myself back, try to keep distance.

These are all very real first-hand experience.

<br>

And then I realize, this would exactly be transferable to Dean and Cas's feelings, probably post-canon. Both of them almost killed the other so many times, 8x17 brainwashed Cas beated Dean up, trained to kill him repeatedly; 10x20 MoC!Dean beated Cas up and just barely an inch away from killing him; godstiel; casifer; demon!Dean; etc etc.

Imagine post-canon, everything is all good and solved, and Cas is back and Dean is alive, and probably established relationship even, but they both have these recurrent PTSD nightmares of killing the other, seeing all the blood in their own hands, seeing them dying again and again in their dreams and it's all their fault, and waking up before they can ask for forgiveness or confession. This will mess them up so badly like it messed me up. After waking up, they'll desperately try to make sure the other is fine and okay, and then too guilty to allow themselves to be around, not trusting themselves to be around or not to make any harm, trying to distance themselves. Undoubtedly causing their relationship to start crumbling. god, it's gonna be so much angst
