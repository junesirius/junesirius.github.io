I got curious about what that whole paragraph of Enochian sigil really means, which Cas tattooed on himself for angel warding the first thing after he became a human.

There are abundant resources about real Enochian as an academic research topic, and I had to admit, I might get a tad obsessed (just as much as Charlie got obsessed with hunting). And I'd really want to do some deciphering or decoding myself.

(But also know that I know practically absolutely nothing about Enochian, and this is well likely just me fumbling around and bullshitting and entertaining myself. Some other disclaimer stuff blabla.)

(Many may have already done such translation ten years ago, but I can't seem to find any now. The closest and my most beloved Enochian reading post on livejournal also seemed to be discontinued just this year, but luckily wayback machine has kept old copies. Still, it seems many resources and information seem to be getting lost.)

A direct Enochian-to-English letter-to-letter alphabetical conversion based on Alistair Crowley (always found this name so interesting)'s version (the alphabet can be found [here](https://skyknowledge.com/enochian.htm)):

> RQA AQ (Lucifer) TQT ING
>
> EN NASU (Lucifer) RZAS SIR
>
> FGHIC BD ZX CU R
>
> EHICL ZFGHI CL NM

The biggest symbol in the center and crosses first two lines is the symbol for Lucifer, which is a little baffling to see Cas tattoos it onto himself.

Most words used in this sigil don't exist in the official Enochian dictionary that is based solely on the original nineteen calls raw material (which is also where I try to base my translation on).

I'd guess this is because sigils are like short-hand writing, words need to be more compressed, so most vowels are skipped, most consonants are compressed together, making it very difficult to understand. (Totally not because of the makeup department in season 9 not doing enough research, especially not as much as they did back then in season 5 when carving the rib sigils, which has full complete decodable meanings.)

So, here's what I presumed the original non-shorthand version of the Enochian should be:

> QA AQ(LO) (Lucifer) T Q IN
>
> EN(AY) NA(ZPS) (Lucifer) (TO)RZ(U) AS SI(AT)R(IS)
>
> G(O)HI(A) B(RG)D(O) Z(I)X(LAY) C UR(AN)
>
> E(T)H(ARZ)I (CI)CL(E) Z (BA)GHI(R) (CI)CL(E) N(OR-)M(OLAP)

And the very direct translation from the above Enochian is:

> Creation in (Lucifer) it mine
>
> Lord sword: (Lucifer) rise was scorpions
>
> we say sleep stirs, unto notsee
>
> peace mysteries: they fury. mysteries: sons of men

Enochian doesn't really have punctuations (at least I couldn't see), but it seems the pattern shared by the makeup department seems to have some kind of punctuations (like the colons and periods), so I'm trying to accommodate them in the translation as well.

So now, further smoothening that message, making more sense of the sentences, this is what I got:

(I'm translating the first "Lucifer" as "light", and the second one as "fall")

> Creation in light that I am
>
> God's sword rises and falls to be creatures on Earth
>
> We fall into sleep and stir, until we are unseen
>
> Let me be a mystery to those who have fury, let me be a mystery to the sons of men

And this, finally does look like a beautifully written warding sigil and spell now.
