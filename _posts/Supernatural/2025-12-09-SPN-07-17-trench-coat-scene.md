finally found 7x17 script!! remember that interview (or podcast?) where Jensen said something about the scene where Dean gives the trench coat back to Cas, but the lines in the script written by Sera is suddenly turning very girly and not something he'd imagine Dean would say, so he had to spend almost an hour with Robert Singer and Misha to come up with a more manly way to deliver it. And this is what's in the script:

> EXT. HOSPITAL - CONTINUOUS
>
> Cas, freaked, walks through the lot. Dean catches up.
>
> **Dean:** Cas. Stop. (Cas does, reluctant) If you remember-- you know you did the best you could at the time--
>
> **Castiel:** Don't defend me. Do you know the death toll? In Heaven, on Earth? (then) We didn't part friends, Dean.
>
> **Dean:** So?
>
> **Castiel:** I deserved to die. I can't possibly fix it-- so why did I walk out of the river?
>
> **Dean:** I dunno. Maybe to go fix it. (then) Look. He goes to the car trunk. Digs... Comes up with Cas's folded, stained TRENCH COAT.
>
> **Dean:** Dumb to keep. I know. I saw you-- dissolve or whatever. But, just in case. 'Cause I never stopped wanting to fix it either. So we got something in common. (re: the coat) Just-- take it. Please.

<br>

Jensen is right, it doesn't feel like the best writing, because... Dean just wouldn't use those words to admit those very specific feeling. In the end they cut the whole speech, leaving just Dean holding out the trench coat (which admittedly is still one of the biggest destiel moments, they just couldn't make it any less mutual pining from that shot and plot design). But still, it's good to know at least that's in his heart. Dean wanted to fix it as much as Cas did.
