It's so very classic Dean to refuse to talk about his *feelings*, but it's also (kinda?) rare to see Sam this straightforwardly asking about Dean's feelings, specifically it's his feelings getting hurt by Cas.

And you know what's crazier to think about? This is from the official script, and the original script has Dean saying "I love you" to Cas just the day before this conversation...

I wonder how much Dean tells Sam when he catches him up about everything that happened in the Crypt.

A little off-topic but some new findings just from typing and reading this part of the script again: Dean had said "Do it! Just do it!" in S05E18 too when Cas was pissed and beating him up. What's the matter, Dean? Always so keen about challenging Cas to kill you?

And the "breaking the connection" part, it is so so clear from the script that it's Dean's words that make him himself again, not the tablet. After Dean said "I love you" (or in the show, "I need you"), he's "hearing him for the first time", and then immediately next shot in Naomi's office, "he's finally himself in this room", which are both before him touching the tablet.

No more questions, you honor. Case closed.
