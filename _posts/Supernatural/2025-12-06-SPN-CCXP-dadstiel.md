Biggest takeaway from watching CCXP Day 1 video: dadstiel is just so amazingly sweet and domestic and awesome, and we need more.

There were just far too many, too sweet Cas/Claire (Misha/Kathryn) moments.

If there's ever a Season 16, we really definitely need more of them together.

Right from the beginning, when they got onto the stage, the audience exploded with screaming and cheering, everyone was waving and greeting, and guess who were the only two people taking out their phones to film the fans.

Yep, like father, like daughter. Cas and Claire started possessing Misha and Kathryn.

Kathryn started it, and then almost immediately, Misha followed suit.

And near the end of their sessions, quoting from Rich, Misha went rogue. Pretty sure that's not part of the hosts' plan, Misha went to the audience to take a fan question.

And almost immediately, Kathryn did the same, jumping off the stage to take another question from the other side of the audience, literally copying Misha's action step by step.

Once again, like Cas and Claire, like father, like daughter. Rogue, as they're called. Or free will to spoil the fans, if put in a different way.

<br>

And more during the quiz/interview/QA time.

The very first question is for Misha:

> **Host:** Castiel has one of the most profound developments in the series. If you could explore another phase of this character, what would you choose to explore?
>
> **Misha:** It would be fun to explore Castiel's relationship with his biological daughter, who we sort of abandoned. They would be an amazing duo, as his daughter, Claire, grows up and becomes a woman, and she would be a great hunter. We could have had a whole epic arc there.

And immediately followed by a story of his interaction with his daughter, and how much Kathryn just reminded him of her.

When Kathryn was asked about how she likes Brazil, coming to the country for the first time, Kathryn said Misha kept telling her she should come.

<br>

And then, when it was Kathryn's turn to answer this question:

> **Host:** If the Supernatural universe were to get a new spin-off, where would you like to see Claire now?
>
> **Kathryn:** I'm sure Claire is out there, still kicking ass, taking names. There's always going to be monsters, and someone is always gonna have to hunt them. So I think she'd still be doing that. And hopefully she's with Cas and Jensen and Jared and the wayward sisters.

"Hopefully Claire would still be hunting, and with Cas, and Winchesters and wayward sisters." Among everyone, Cas is the very first she'd like to be together with.

That suddenly reminded me how much the show's original ending missed Claire and everyone else's reaction about Cas's death, and then Dean's. We didn't get a single passing line about what Claire would be like when she hears about everything that happened, and that Cas was gone, if Dean and Sam would tell her the full story.

If there were ever a season 16, we really totally need that reunion scene.

<br>

Question about Claire's birthday gift from Cas:

> **Host:** What did Cas give Claire for her birthday? (Misha very quickly raises his hand, and then Kathryn raises hers too)
>
> **Misha:** (lowers his hand, and to Kathryn) You know, you know.
>
> **Kathryn:** (very happily, confidently) I got this one. Grumpy cat.

Of course she'll remember the grumpy cat, because --

Right when asked about the most memorable/impactful scene, that's practically the episode and the scene that got mentioned:

> **Host:** Kathryn, what was the episode that made a mark on you, that will forever be your favorite?
>
> **Kathryn:** That's so hard. I think my favorite episode is the one -- There's one episode that I was in, with Misha, and you know that guy, Jensen (laugh). And it's something -- they were in front of the car. (to Misha) Do you remember that episode? In front of the car? You guys are like sending me off my way, but you're like, "Go be a fighter, but also don't." But then you all are like, "We know you're gonna be a fighter," and "We'll have your back, if you ever get in trouble." Remember that?
>
> **Misha:** I do remember it.
>
> **Kathryn:** I think I was pretty young when I shot that. And I think just being there at that age with them, left a big impact on me. And I'll never forget it. And I love that episode.

<br>

And then, actually I think that first fan question is absolutely the cherry on the very top of the whole session:

> **Fan:** Jack never interacted with Claire. What do you guys think would be the family dynamic between Cas, Claire, and Jack?
>
> **Misha:** Awww, sweet question.
>
> **Kathryn:** Ah yes, I never met Jack. I kind of always wanted to meet him. I always thought there would be something there, right?
>
> **Misha:** I think there would probably be something like sibling rivalry there.
>
> **Kathryn:** We'd be competitive.
>
> **Misha:** You'd be competitive.
>
> **Kathryn:** I'd try to play football with him and be like, "What's football?"
>
> **Misha:** Yeah. But you'd take him out, for sure.

Just a sweet, sweet family moment. Beautiful, intimate domestic scene. Cas with two kids, raising Claire and Jack like they are just two very normal kids, and Claire being Jack's big sister. Aww now I completely got sucked into the dadstiel trope (and even though DILF isn't my usual thing, I can totally see where it comes from now).

In any case, it's so good to see Misha and Kathryn interact so well and close, and now it's time for me to get some Cas with Claire story to read.
