### draft notes

> Because even though Wednesday is literally the tunnel at the end of my light, I can't imagine my life without her in it.
>
> -- Enid Sinclair

I see destiel in there

I see gelphie in there

I see WildeHopps in there

and now I have Wenclair

I mean, what's the chance that all the ships can devour me like they are one? there's always one (or both) sacrificing themselves for another, always some breakup arc and heartbroken (or worse, widower arc), someone almost dying, someone scared to shit, always some choosing the other over themselves, and love too much but not realizing the feeling...

I mean, I didn't watch many shows or movies this year, let me see, this year it's just spn (for the 100th time), Wicked 2, Zootopia 2, and now just finished Wednesday 2, and ALL OF THEM manage to make me cry at least once and remind me of the other ships? what's the issue with me? now I have to wonder if there's something deeper in me that made me keep looking for and getting attracted by the same story again and again...

(no actually, I have the answer already, the story of those who don't belong and are lonely, those who still care and love, sometimes too much, those who don't believe they'll be loved back and accepted, but eventually there's always someone who does, someone who see you and love you back, or even more, or die loving. no matter how different you are, they understand, and they accept, and they love. I feel like I'm just trying over and over again to heal myself in those stories, trying to make myself believe these aren't just stories, that one day I'll be seen and loved like those stories do. that's why I too am writing the same type of story over and over again, in different worlds, on different characters, but always the same core, because that's what I am. that's what I'm waiting for. that's what I'm looking for.)
